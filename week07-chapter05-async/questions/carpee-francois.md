If you push back on every small thing your manager does, how can you be an easy person to work with? Can you find a balance so that you can decide which things are worth battling so that your manager learns your pattern without being labeled as someone who complains about everything? 

The chapter says to push back on things you don't like immediately but now everything is worth a confrontation. Navigating this can be tricky.
